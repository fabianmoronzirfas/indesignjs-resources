Selected List of InDesign Scripting Resources

Welcome to InDesign Scripting Resources

I try to add and comment all valuable websites, repositories and sources linked to InDesign Scripting with ExtendScript. The resources in the categories are in alphabetical ordner. Feel free to add your site or any other resource you know. Pleas file an issues or pull request, I will add the resource as fast as possible.

Meta

  • [EN] InDesignSecrets has lots of articles about scripts. The overview page is outdated.
  • [EN] Kasyans long list of scripts.
  • [EN] Smashing Magazine has a shiny selection of valuable scripts.
